Calories Tracker 🍏
A lightweight and intuitive Android application designed to help users monitor their daily nutrition, calculate calorie needs, and track macronutrients (proteins, fats, and carbohydrates).
🚀 Key Features
Daily Food Diary: Log meals into specific categories: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner, and Snacks.
Smart Calculations: Automatically determines your daily calorie and macro goals using the Mifflin-St Jeor formula based on your weight, height, age, and activity level.
Dynamic Visualizations:
PieChart: Real-time breakdown of your macro distribution for the day.
BarChart: Historical view of your calorie intake over the last 7 days.
Extensive Food Database: Built-in catalog with detailed descriptions, health tips, and nutritional facts for common foods.
Calendar Integration: Select any date to view past logs or plan ahead.
Persistent Storage: All data is saved locally on your device using Room Database, ensuring your progress is never lost.
🛠 Tech Stack
Language: Kotlin
Database: Room (SQLite) with KSP for efficient data management.
UI/UX: XML Layouts, ViewBinding, and Material Design Components.
Charts: MPAndroidChart for sleek, interactive data visualization.
JSON Parsing: Gson for handling the product catalog.
